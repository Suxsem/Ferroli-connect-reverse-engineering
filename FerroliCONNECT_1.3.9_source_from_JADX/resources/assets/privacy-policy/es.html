Privacy policy

1.Personal Information and Its Use

Cloudwarm gathers two types of information about users:

(1) Non-personally identifiable information:
When visitors come to our web site or App, we collect and aggregate information indicating,which functions were visited, operation steps, temperature changes etc.Collecting such information involves the logging of IP addresses, operating system and browser software etc. used by each visitor to Cloudwarm App and web site. Although such information is not personally identifiable, we can determine from the IP address a visitor's Internet Service Provider and the geographic location of his or her point of connectivity. The non-personally identifiable information we collect helps us identify the most popular areas of our App or web site and determine the effectiveness of our promotional activities.

(2) Personally identifiable information:
In certain areas of the Cloudwarm App or web site -for example, while registering for the Cloudwarm App service a user is asked to provide personal information such as his/her address and phone number.
This is to improve our services in an easier and better way. Your address helps us to analyse the outside temperature where Cloudwarm is being installed. Phone information allows us to offer a better service to you.
